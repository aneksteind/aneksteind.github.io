Resume

NTL: 2023 - Present

I run a company, along with my co-founder, specializing in iOS development and consulting. Our flagship product is called Reflect, an iOS toolkit for self-improvement and self-discovery.

Immunant: 2022 - 2024

At Immunant, I focused on C2Rust's static and dynamic analysis tools to transform unsafe Rust code into safe code, while also teaching Google’s Comprehensive Rust course to Google’s engineering teams.

Dexai Robotics: 2020 - 2022

I implemented the entire robot task planning system and integrated it with the rest of the services necessary for the robot to serve food to people. You can see it in action in various videos online. I also developed a critical subsystem of the product end-to-end, the bowl retrieval mechanism, in collaboration with the mechanical team.

Boston Engineering: 2019 - 2020

I worked on a medical device built around an nRF52840 SoC, integrating sensors and cameras, implementing state machines, and developing algorithms to ensure the device functioned properly under tight memory and latency constraints.

Systems and Technology Research: 2018 - 2019

I was the primary software engineer for a multi-million dollar program, the goal of which was to create autonomous agents for remediating botnets. The domain was modeled as a POMDP, and I implemented POMDP solvers such as POMCP and enhanced the accuracy of the agent’s belief state particle generation with Gibbs sampling over a Baysian Network that modeled the probability space of its world state.
